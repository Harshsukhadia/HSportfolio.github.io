01 — About

A bit about me

I'm a Computer Engineering graduate from India, now doing my M.Sc. in Computational Science at Laurentian University in Sudbury. My coursework has ranged from machine learning and neural networks to image processing, cybersecurity, and applied cryptography — which is a weird and fun combination.

At Perfecti.io I'm building a Python pipeline from scratch that takes big messy mortgage PDF packages and figures out what's inside them — splitting pages, classifying government IDs, detecting barcodes, the whole thing. Before that I spent time at a clean tech startup working on AI chatbots, did a WordPress contract build for an ISO consultancy, and spent a couple of years writing production PHP/Laravel at a software shop in India.

I also spent about eight months mentoring students in AI and ML through Intel's AI for Youth program — got to attend national events in Delhi and Bangalore, which was a highlight.
